Our Story

Welcome to Le Gourmet, where culinary artistry meets exceptional dining.

Established in 1995 by renowned Chef Marcus Lindström, Le Gourmet has become Stockholm's premier destination for fine dining. Our two Michelin stars reflect our unwavering commitment to culinary excellence.

Our Philosophy

We believe in creating unforgettable dining experiences through innovative cuisine that honors Swedish traditions while embracing global influences. Each dish tells a story of passion, precision, and the finest ingredients.

Awards & Recognition

  • Two Michelin Stars (2015-Present)
  • Best Restaurant in Scandinavia 2022
  • Wine Spectator Grand Award 2023
  • Nordic Prize for Culinary Excellence 2021
